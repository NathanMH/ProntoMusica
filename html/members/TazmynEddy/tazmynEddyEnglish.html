TAZMYN EDDY

    Tazmyn Eddy obtained his Bachelor of Music degree from the University of Victoria, British Columbia, followed by an Artist Diploma from Le Conservatoire de Musique de Montréal, Quebec. His principal teachers include Manon Lafrance, Louis Ranger, Tom Shorthouse, Larry Larson, and Stéphane Beaulac.

    Taz has had the pleasure of performing both as a member and as a featured soloist with a number of professional ensembles. In 2009, he was the featured soloist for the Canada West Chamber Orchestras west coast tour, performing Haydns Concerto for Eb trumpet. He also performed as a featured soloist with the University of Victoria Orchestra in January of 2011 after winning their annual concerto competition. He was a member of the National Youth Orchestra of Canada for their 2011, 2012, and 2013 seasons, where he was chosen to perform the famous principal trumpet part for Gustav Mahlers fifth symphony. Other performance experience includes Les Grands Ballets Canadiennes de Montréal, Vancouver Opera, Orchestre Symphonique de Québec, Orchestre Symphonique de Laval, Orchestre Symphonique de Longueuil, National Academy Orchestra, Victoria Symphony, Raven Baroque Ensemble, Victoria Civic Orchestra, Theatre Under the Stars, and the Opera Appassionato Opera Company.
