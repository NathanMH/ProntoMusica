Nadege Wary

    Nadège Wary holds degrees from the Conservatories of Lausanne and Geneva (Switzerland). After completing her Masters in Violin Pedagogy in Lausanne, she then received a Masters in Performance in Geneva under the tutelage of Patrick Genet. While in Switzerland, she performed with numerous orchestras, notably as a substitute in the Orchestre de la Suisse Romande. Though she is originally a violinist, she chose to learn the viola as well and now participates in many projects as a violist.

    After her musical studies in Switzerland, Nadège came to Montreal to complete a Graduate Diploma in Cultural Management, which she received in 2013. She is now the Music Program Coordinator of Share the Warmth, a community organisation in Point-Saint-Charles, where she both teaches and directs an intensive El Sistema-inspired music program for children.
