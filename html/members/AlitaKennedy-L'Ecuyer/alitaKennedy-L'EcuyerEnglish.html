ALITA KENNEDY-L'ECUYER

    Finalist in the Rising Stars Competition in the Ottawa International Chamber Music Festival, Alita Kennedy LEcuyer is a Montreal-based bassoonist with a wide range of musical interests. Her wind quintet, Kaleïo, is the Laureate of the Pentaèdre Chamber Competition and performs regularly with the Allegra Chamber Concert Series.

    Alita has performed with various ensembles, such as the Société des Orchestres de Montreal, the Agora Symphony Orchestra, the McGill Symphony Orchestra and the National Academy Orchestra. She has also performed various operas with the McGill Opera and Stu&Jess Productions. Ms. Kennedy LEcuyer is also greatly interested in contemporary music and has participated in the International New Music Festival in Darmstadt, Germany.
