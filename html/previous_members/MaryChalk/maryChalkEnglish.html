Mary Chalk

    Hailing from Montreal, Mary Chalk has been Second Bassoon in the Orchestre Symphonique de Longueuil since 2013. A sought-after orchestral musician, she has performed with the Orchestre Symphonique de Montréal, the Winnipeg Symphony Orchestra, and the Orchestre Symphonique de Sherbrooke, amongst others.

    She received a Bachelor of Music from The Juilliard School, where she studied with Dr. Frank Morelli. In 2013, Chalk co-composed and performed DotEnter, a multi-disciplinary work which was featured as the opening to the 5 th IMC World Forum on Music in Brisbane, Australia. As a part of the Juilliard Opera program, she participated in the North American premiere of Peter Maxwell Davies' “Kommilitonen!” in 2011.

    She has spent summers at the Orford Arts Centre, Académie Domaine Forget and the Banff Arts Centre. In 2010, she performed in a masterclass for Gustavo Nuñez that was used for the launch of “playwithapro.com”, an online catalogue of video tutorials for winds and brass. Chalk is currently pursuing an Artist Diploma under Stéphane Lévesque at McGill University’s Schulich School of Music.
